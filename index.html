My Personal Interests

I love to rock climb. I started when I was in my freshman year of high school. I joined a team at the nearby climbing gym, and I began my rock climbing journey. I continue to take climbing classes to learn new techniques and buld my strength.

Art is one of my favorite hobbies. Anytime I feel stressed or need a pick me up I just sit down and start a new artwork. I like to paint landscapes of places I would want to go, like a flower filled meadow or a lushious rain forest.

I also love new adventures. I try to lead a life of trying new and exciting things. I have been ice climbing, parasailing, sky diving, snowshoeing, snowboarding, cross country skiing, sea kayaking, backpacking, and many other things. One of the most exotic things I did was building a snow cave up in the mountains. I got to camp out inside of it and it was surprisingly warm.
